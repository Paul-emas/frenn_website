How credit line works

Onboarding to the Application

Frenn credit line will be made available to you via our communication channels or in the Application, after fulfilling the necessary requirements.

We will request for certain details from you, such as your name, date of birth, BVN, phone number, etc. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy for how we manage Personal Information collected from you. You also agree to our use of the details you provided, from your Equipment or other data banks for the purpose of guaranteeing your credit line access.

Credit Line Access

You can access a credit line in our Application.We will give you a credit limit from which you can utilise as presented in your downloaded Frenn Application. You can then borrow up to that amount and continue utilising it, as long as you repay what you borrow. There is a fixed repayment schedule for each utilisation. The type of information we will request from you and the extent of it depends on the credit limit you will be able to access. Any credit line accessed and utilised from the Application is deemed authorised by you, unless you have notified us of fraud within a reasonable time.

Credit Line Evaluation

Once you are signed up on our Application, we will conduct our internal checks, assessments and procedures, and you will be informed of our decision of a credit line offer to you. The amount you have access to is dependent on several factors, such as your credit score and verifiable income. The credit limit is constantly monitored and adjusted according to your risk profiling.

Based on our assessment of your creditworthiness, we will present an offer of a credit limit to you which will give you access to a credit line that you can utilise in accordance with the terms provided on your Frenn app account.

Frenn reserves the right to approve or decline a credit line access even if you had previously been granted a credit line. We hold the right to change the terms of any credit line agreement depending on our evaluation of the creditworthiness of each borrower.

As part of the review process, we may request your data from third parties such as Credit Bureaus, you may also be required to provide certain information (such as a bank statements/statements of account) for our evaluation.

You can request to utilise your credit line up to the credit limit as presented to you in the Application. We will subsequently show you stipulated terms for each utilisation based on our evaluation via the Application.

The fee terms may differ for each utilisation of the credit line. The particulars of any successful credit line utilisation –principal amount, interest payable, repayment due dates or any other applicable fees or charges, will be provided on the Application or on request or by other means of communication provided, and such particulars will be based on Frenn’s evaluation of your credit profile.

Loan servicing

Auto Debit: Our primary repayment option for your loan is through our auto-debit feature, which is an automated repayment model using direct debits from your linked auto-debit bank account/card.

Alternate Repayment Models: We will also provide you with alternative options for repayments, such as cash payments via a service provider or other bank branch, or a one-time bank transfer.

If you elect to use any of our alternative repayment models, we will provide you with a unique, customer-specific repayment account (NUBAN) which must be used for the purpose of your repayments.

Overpayments: In the event that you mistakenly overpay in the process of repaying a loan all overpaid amounts shall be treated as pre-payments towards your next due date. In the event of an overpayment with no outstanding balance remaining, upon provision of due evidence of the same to the satisfaction of our Customer Support team, we will provide you with the applicable refund within a reasonable time. Transaction costs might apply and be deducted from the overpayment amount returned to your account.

Full repayment: If you wish to repay your entire outstanding balance before the due date in one lump sum, you are required to contact us at help@frenn.com in order for us to prepare the terms for such repayment. We will provide you with these modified repayment terms and you will be required to accept the terms prior to making any such repayment.
