If, after our comprehensive review, a decision is made to approve your application for the Frenn BNPL service, you will receive a notice (on the Application and by email) of the approval, the approved Credit Amount, and the Repayment Plan.

Where your application is granted as received, Frenn will post the Credit Amount on your Account and proceed to pay the relevant Merchant Partner for the goods and/or services you have ordered, without you needing to make any immediate payment. If you have not ordered any goods or services yet but have simply pre-applied for the Frenn BNPL service to finance your shopping, the approved Credit Amount will be posted on your Account and may be used by you to pay for goods and services whenever you need to.

Frenn is not under an obligation to grant approval for the full amount requested by you.
